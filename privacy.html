Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

HumanDogs Shorts uses the Meta (Facebook/Instagram) Graph API to publish and manage short video content on Instagram on behalf of authorized business accounts. We only access the minimum data necessary to automate safe and reliable posting.

Data We Access

  • Connected Facebook Page and Instagram Business Account identifiers (Page ID, IG User ID).
  • Permissions to upload and publish reels or photos through the Meta Graph API.

What We Do Not Do

  • We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with any third party.
  • We do not collect or store passwords.
  • We do not retain user content longer than necessary for automated posting or analytics.

Security

All access tokens and API credentials are securely stored on our server-side environment. No sensitive data is ever exposed, logged, or included in source control repositories.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions, please contact us at humandogs.official@gmail.com.
